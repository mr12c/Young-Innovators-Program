    Event History

    The Young Innovator's Program serves as a distinguished platform dedicated to fostering the scientific curiosity and problem-solving acumen of aspiring young minds.


    Eligibility Criteria: This competition exclusively welcomes students currently enrolled in grades VIII to XII, with the explicit objective of acknowledging and nurturing their creative potential. We implore you not to allow your exceptional ideas to languish in dormancy.
